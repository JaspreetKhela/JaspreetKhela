Welcome! On this webpage, you will find general information about me, links to my work-related social media profiles, and an overview of my professional, academic, and personal development history and pursuits.

About Me


I am a Toronto-based (full-stack) software developer (for Web2, Android, and iOS applications), data engineer, data analyst, and data scientist. I am in the process of becoming a cybersecurity engineer, cybersecurity (white hat) hacker, financial engineer, blockchain engineer, user experience/interface designer, and digital marketer, project manager, and product manager.

I am currently open to pursuing opportunities in software development, data engineering, data analytics, and data science offered by start-ups, scale-ups, and enterprises within North America.

Academic, Professional, and Personal Competencies


The following list of competencies that I have or seek to develop facilitate my spiritual, physical, intellectual, relational, and emotional growth in an effort to increase my antifragility, my understanding of the fundamental aspects of modern society, and developing my competencies in a multitude of domains.


In the short-term, I am in the process of developing my academic competencies in
  • (theoretical and applied) mathematics,
  • (theoretical, applied, and experimental) physics,
  • (theoretical and applied) computer science,
  • (theoretical and applied) statistics,
  • (theoretical and applied) economics, and
  • (theoretical, applied, and experimental) chemistry.
In the long-term, I aspire to become a research mathematician specializing in mathematical physics and mathematical medicine. I also have a peripheral interest in performing academic research in the areas of quantum computing, machine learning, cryptography, nuclear fusion, and finance.


In the long-term, I will develop my professional competencies in
  • military engineering (regarding the construction of field defences and obstacles; construction of roads, airfields, and helicopter landing sites; construction of rafts and bridges; radio communications for combat arms operations; mathematics, military writing, basic military organization, and military history; personal camouflaging and camouflaging of section positions; operation of troop weapons and section defensive tasks and procedures; basic procedures of rigging and firing of explosive charges, mine laying, and booby-trapping; use and care of section tools and safety equipment; combat diving operations; soil analysis operations, explosive ordnance disposal operations; instructional techniques; water supply operations; power boat operations; and heavy equipment operations),

  • skilled trades (regarding construction-, agrarian-, and emergency services-related trades),
  • multimedia production (regarding audio, graphics, photography, video, and visual effects production),
  • performance arts (regarding screenwriting, dance, and theatrical arts), and
  • business development (regarding entrepreneurship, business innovation, business analysis, project management, process improvement, finance, accounting, and business management).


In the long-term, I will develop my personal competencies in
  • culinary, baking, and pastery arts;
