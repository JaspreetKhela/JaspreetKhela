Professional, Academic, and Personal Development History and Pursuits Overview


Welcome! On this webpage, you will find general information about me and links to my work-related social media profiles.

I am a Toronto-based (full-stack) software developer (for web, Android, and iOS applications).

I am currently open to pursuing opportunities in software development, data analytics and science, and cybersecurity offered by start-ups, scale-ups, and enterprises within North America.

In the short-term, I am in the process of developing my academic competencies in (theoretical and applied) mathematics, (theoretical, applied, and experimental) physics, (theoretical and applied) computer science, (theoretical and applied) economics, (theoretical, applied, and experimental) chemistry, and (theoretical and applied) statistics.

In the long-term, I will develop my professional competencies in hardware development (with respect to electronics, electromechanics, programmable logic controllers, and robotics development), skilled trades (with respect to construction-, agrarian-, and emergency services-related trades), multimedia production (with respect to sound, graphics, photography, video, and visual effects production), performance arts (with respect to screenwriting, dance, and theatrical arts), and business development (with respect to entrepreneurship, business innovation, business analysis, project management, process improvement, finance, accounting, and business management).

In the long-term, I will develop my personal competencies in: culinary, baking, and pastery arts; combat sports (with respect to Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, Wrestling, Muay Thai, Boxing, and weapons training); mindset and human condition education; and languages (with respect to English, French, Spanish, Hindi, Punjabi, Arabic, Mandarin, and Latin).
